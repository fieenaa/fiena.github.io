UITM MERBOK

In a small town, a cheerful girl named Nur Sufina Binti Ayub with a nickname Fina, attended Sekolah Rendah Kebangsaan Peremba. I loved music, playing in the school garden, and participating in the annual play. With nurturing teachers and close friendships, my primary school years became a cherished chapter in my life, inspiring me love for learning. During secondary school, I joined the foot marching. I practiced diligently and, on parade day, performed flawlessly. The crowd cheered us with confidence and precision. It was a moment of pride and unity, highlighting the value of teamwork and dedication. While at university, I took an Information Management course. There are various knowledge that I got while taking this course. Even though there are subjects that are very difficult to understand, I am lucky to have lecturers and friends who help me when I'm having difficulties and confusion.
